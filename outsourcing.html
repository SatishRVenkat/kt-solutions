Outsourcing

Outsourcing executes projects at the milestone level to support incremental project components and overall project success. KT-Solutions allows flexible organizations to unite outsourcing services with process consultation and transformation solutions. The combined approach helps our technical experts closely associate with client teams for maximum performance.


In order to achieve tight ‘resource to project cost’ ratios, global organizations seek partners like KT-Solutions to deliver services with world-class standards and seamless techniques. Our process-based approach provides extreme flexibility so that clients can outsource single modules or complete application development projects.


We understand that resource management over a range of projects is crucial for emerging organizations. KT-Solutions provides the qualified personnel to work on-site or off-site, on-shore or off-shore to meet client specifications, on time and on budget.


With diverse IT industry knowledge and service expertise, we efficiently deliver fixed-price projects as well as extended time modules (ETM). Both private and public sector clients appreciate our high standards for project excellence and unbeatably low prices to achieve projects of this caliber.
